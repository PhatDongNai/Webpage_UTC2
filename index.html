Exploring the Vibrant UTC2 Campus: A Hub of Learning and Innovation

The University of Transport and Communications - Campus in Ho Chi Minh City (UTC2) is a vibrant educational hub. It features the E10 building, Friendship Park, and a state-of-the-art multi-purpose lecture hall. The campus is also home to the Integration Road - Flower Road, and the E1 corridor, a place of nostalgia for many UTC2 students. The library caters to students’ academic needs with its extensive collection of resources.

Modern Facilities: UTC2 boasts state-of-the-art facilities such as the multi-purpose lecture hall and the E10 building.

Vibrant Environment: The campus is home to various landmarks like the Friendship Park and the Integration Road - Flower Road, creating a vibrant and engaging environment for students.

Academic Resources: The library at UTC2 is well-equipped with a wide range of academic resources, supporting students’ learning and research needs effectively.
